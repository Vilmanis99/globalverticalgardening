Everyone is concerned with eating healthy and organic these days by growing their own herbs to use for cooking and adding more flavour.  Everyone is trying to avoid sodium, sugar, and fat, therefore, a great way to assure freshness and quality is to grow herbs ourselves and a good way to start in that direction is with herb garden kits. Gardening kits are successful gardener weapon and they normally includes a plastic or peat tray, sometimes divided into sections, some potting soil, herbal seeds, a clear plastic dome or cover, and many more. Some of these herb gardening kits are quite new and use technology like hydroponics to grow larger herbs faster.

Herb type gardening kits are relatively easy to find. Most hardware stores, home centres, discount stores, nurseries, and super centres carry them. The contents of the kits will vary. Some will include seeds, while others will not. The hydroponic herb garden kits don't have seeds at all, but rather include pre-made growing pods that you just drop into the machine and wait for it to grow.

Many people assume that the more expensive the kit, the better it will work. This isn't always true. The more expensive kits are usually the more high-tech kits. But if you are just looking for something small and simple, then there is no need to spend the money on the expensive high-tech hydroponic kit. You can simply go for one of the small traditional kits.

Herbal garden kits vary greatly in size as well. Some contain one small pot that can sit on your counter, while others contain several pots that will require a larger amount of space. [**Garden Tower is a unique kit**](https://www.globalverticalgardening.com/product-review-1/gardentower2review) with specially designed pots that stack on top of one another while still allowing plants to grow unobstructed. This type of herbal garden kit would be great for someone wanting an herb garden in their kitchen, but don't have a lot of room.
