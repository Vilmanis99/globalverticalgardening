## Buyers Guide

#### How to Make Compost Tea

Unlike the home tea, the brewing process of compost tea is not simple. It goes beyond putting compost into a teabag and leaving it to brew. This is because compost contains both beneficial and harmful microorganisms. Beneficial microorganisms need oxygenated to survive in your compost tea. Therefore, you should use the ideal compost for quality compost tea. This should have low or no fungi, but rich in bacteria. If you are DIY enthusiast, here is [how to build a DIY compost tea brewer](https://nourishandnestle.com/make-compost-tea-brewer-brew-compost-tea/).

#### Things to Consider When Buying Compost Tea Brewer

Here are some factors to consider if you want to buy the best compost tea brewer for your needs:

- **Capacity**

One of the most important feature to look out for in a compost tea brewer is capacity. The capacity determines the amount of water and brewed solution it will hold. However, the capacity varies from small to large models. For home use, go for compost tea brewers with a small capacity, and brewers with large gallon capacity for commercial use.

- **Price**

Your budget is a vital factor when buying compost tea brewer. If you are looking for a brewer to use to cover a small [organic garden](https://www.globalverticalgardening.com/organic-gardenings), then you should not spend so much. On the contrary, a commercial compost tea brewer is expensive. Remember, some of these units cost thousands of dollars.

- **Size**

Size and capacity are associated. Therefore, if you want a compost tea brewer with a larger capacity, go for one with a larger size. For home use, go for a brewer with small-sized gallons.

- **Weight**

Weight is another important thing to consider, because the brewers have different size and shape. Some have lightweight, while others are heavy. In addition, depending the size and capacity, the kit can be heavy when you add water. Weight determines if your chosen compost tea brewer is portable or not.

#### FAQ About Compost Tea Brewer

- **What is Compost Tea?**

It is a rich microbial solution made from nutrients and microorganisms extracts present in aerated compost. This solution re-energizes your plants and soil.

- **How easy are compost tea brewers in use?**

Compost tea brewers are normally easier to use. The measurements do not always need to be precise. In fact, the brewing process of compost tea is similar to that of brewing tea at home. All you need is one of the best compost tea brewers, add your ingredients and leave it to brew. However, you need to connect the pump to take in oxygen for quality brew.

- **Can I use My Compost to Make Compost Tea?**

Yes. However, during [composting](https://www.globalverticalgardening.com/composting/what-is-composting), ensure you aerate you compost through occasion turning. Besides that, leave it for around 6-8 weeks for important bacteria and fungi to grow. Enough temperature is vital to kill harmful fungi.
