Who doesn't love to add pretty flowers to decorate his indoor garden? Green leafy plants are a blessing in disguise for indoor gardeners. There unique shapes, attractive arrangements, and ferny leaves make your garden look gorgeous. However, there is a difference in growing plants and getting some flowers out of it. These blooming flowers will brighten your home and add a splash of color to your indoor garden. If you desire to add flourishing flowers to your garden, you are in the right place.

## Here is the list of top 10 flowering houseplants that will bloom your indoor gardening.

### Hibiscus Houseplant

This tropical flowering plant has a lovely arrangement of five big petals. These plants come in a variety of colors like violet, mauve, white, purple, red, or blue. If these plants are provided with sufficient light, they continue to bloom freely from late spring till the fall. These can be placed next to a sunny window in your indoor garden. A centered long pistol along with wide petals gives these plants a unique appearance. These amazing flowers usually last one or two days, but they continue to decor your garden throughout the season.

![Top 10 Flowering Houseplants That Will Bloom Your Indoor Garden](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/hibiscus.jpg)

### African Violets

African violets need a nominal amount of indirect light and a moderately warm temperature, thus making these flowers quite effortless to grow. These are usually small plants and their flowers come in a wide range of styles and colors. One has to be careful while watering these houseplants as the leaves can rot on getting too much moisture. The passionate lovers of this plant try repotting process when these violets get too big for their original pots. This technique helps them to bloom more readily throughout the year.

![african violet](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/African-Violet2.jpg)

### Christmas Cactus

Christmas cactus yields pink, red, orange and white flowers in the winter season. This flowering succulent has paddle-like foliage. For the flower lovers, Christmas cactus is a delightful extension to their indoor garden for colder seasons, apart from the rest that bloom in summer and spring. Due to its segmented and flattened stems, these flowering plants give a distinguished look to your vertical garden even when they are not in bloom.

![christmas cactus](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/christmas-cactus.jpg)

### Impatiens

Also renowned as busy Lizzies, Impatiens are thirsty houseplants that need plenty of water and generous bright light for blooming. A blooming Impatient plant grows the flower of different colors that can be orange, red, pink, violet and white. Optimum conditions like direct sunlight or a temperature above 52-55 F helps them grow indoor, for all around the year. Mostly, they do not thrive in cold temperatures.

![impatients](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/impatients.jpg)

### Lipstick Plant

Lipstick is among a few of the most loved flowering houseplants. These can be grown inside a greenhouse or within your garden. Setting these vining houseplants in hanging baskets or atop a side table will give them a great look. They need a bright room with a humid atmosphere and a steady warm temperature for blooming flowers. Before the opening of flowers, the buds develop from their tubes as red lipstick thus making them a unique addition to your indoor garden. They give a pleasing effect to the garden owners as well.

![lipstick](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/lipstick.jpg)

### Peace Lily Houseplant

Peace lily houseplant is extremely easy to grow thus making it the most common houseplant that blooms. They are best at purifying pollutants from the air and for this reason; they serve as a marvelous addition to your indoor garden. They can flourish in low-light as well as shady areas. They seldom have difficulties with pests. Its flowers are white and can last for months upon blooming.

![peace lily](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/peace-lily.jpg)

### Bromeliads Houseplant

Bromeliads are incredible tropical indoor plants and are best known for their long-lasting flowers and colorful foliage. These flowering plants can flourish in low lights and without much care. It is sometimes fun to grow bromeliads as they thrive with great ease. You just have to fill the center of the vase with water and keep it fresh. Believe it or not, bromeliads will decorate your interior a great deal!

![bromeliads](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/bromeliads.jpg)

### Begonias

There are a variety of begonias plants that can be kept in your indoor garden. Normally considered to be outdoor houseplants, they have all the attributes to enhance the elegance of your indoor garden. They are undemanding, strong and free-flowering plants. A simple pot of this flowering plant can please the owner with its charming foliage. Its innumerable varieties incorporate the bushy or trailing varieties and different styles of foliage begonias.

![begonias](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/begonias.jpg)

### White Jasmine

Jasmine is one of the prettiest flowering plants you can add to your indoor garden. Its white-colored, star-shaped flowers and tiny leaves are a delight to watch in any garden. These aromatic vines bloom in winter when they are placed in bright sunlight light for a few hours. At the same time, they require cooler temperatures to bloom. Thus, these plants are climate dependent. Most of the varieties of Jasmine flower in warmer areas whereas for cold areas, JasminumPolyanthum should be considered. Due to their climbing nature, their growth is supported by providing them with trellis.

![Top 10 Flowering Houseplants That Will Bloom Your Indoor Garden — illustration 9](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/image-asset.jpeg)

### Moth Orchids

These are amazingly graceful and elegant looking plants. Moth Orchids give yellow, pink, or white flowers on the top of the broad leaves. High humidity and indirect sunlight are the optimum conditions for its blooming. No doubt they are beautiful plants to add to your indoor garden, however, you need to take special care of these in comparison to other plants. They should never be overwatered. Direct exposure to sunlight can burn its leaves so they need ample light to bloom.

![blossom orchid plant](/images/top-10-flowering-houseplants-that-will-bloom-your-indoor-garden/blossom-orchid-plant.png)

### Bottom Line

Cultivating such an exceptional flower bed will not only decorate your indoor garden, but the aromatic characteristics of these flowers will also give you a pleasant feeling. Flowering plants has always been a lovely feeling and a great way to keep you surrounded by fresh flowers.
