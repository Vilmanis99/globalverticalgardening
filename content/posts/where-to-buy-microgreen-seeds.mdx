The whole thing about microgreens is fascinating.  Their nutritional value of microgreens is not something you can ignore. In fact, according to research, microgreen leaves are four to six times more nutritious than the mature ones. Apart from consuming them, you can also invest in microgreen farming. Microgreens are harvested about 10-14 days after germination. That means they have the fastest ROI. You can sell them in the markets, restaurants, and to your neighbors.

Microgreens make vibrant salads, garnishes, sandwiches, and soups. Every journey begins with one step. As long as you have a ready structure to shelter your plants, you can go ahead and plant your microgreen seeds or organic seeds, but do you know where to buy them?There are a variety of seeds going for different prices, and there is an array of stores that sell the seeds including Amazon. Let’s explore the stores where you can find microgreen or organic seeds if you want to grow microgreens at home.
