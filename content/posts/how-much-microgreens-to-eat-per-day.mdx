## How Much Microgreens Should You Eat Per Day?

The ideal daily intake of microgreens depends on your health goals, but a **general ballpark** amount is:

- **1–2 handfuls per day** (about **25–50 grams** or **1–2 cups loosely packed**)
- For smoothies or juices: **1 small handful** (~20 grams) blends easily without overpowering taste.
- For salads: **1–2 handfuls** makes a great nutrient boost.
- If you're aiming for specific benefits (like higher Vitamin C), you can go up to **60–70 grams** per day — still very safe for most people.

**Quick Tip:** Microgreens are so nutrient-dense that even a small amount packs big health benefits. It's better to eat them regularly than worry about exact grams every day.
