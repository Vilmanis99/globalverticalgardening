When the weather prevents you from going outside to enjoy some gardening, it's just as beneficial and effective to do some indoor gardening instead. In this way, you can indulge in some gardening all year round, while still keeping warm and dry. Better yet, your plants also keep warm and out of harm way from frosts, slugs and deer! By the end of reading this article, you will have many indoor gardening ideas and tips that will have you well on your way to growing lots of wonderful indoor plants and flowers.

Indoor gardening will allow you to have much more flexibility when growing certain types of plants that need a specific temperature. For example, you could grow whatever vegetables you feel like all year round, and not have to wait until they are in season to enjoy their benefits. It also allows you to grow them organically to your own personal requirements.

Indoor gardens are similar to outdoor gardens, it's just that you are caring for plants in your home instead of in your garden. A big plus to indoor gardening is that it can be enjoyed by people that live in the cities with no access to land. It is also a joyful hobby for people that are confined indoors. The real bonus is that they are healthy for us. Basically plants take in carbon dioxide and turn it into oxygen (photosynthesis) thereby purifying the air that we breath. So you have attractive plants with added benefits.

## *How to Choose Plants for Your Indoor Garden?*

The most important thing to remember when you want to begin an indoor garden is that you purchase the right kind of plant and / or plants.

Whether you want to transform the overall look of your home, whether you simply have no space outdoors, or whether you just want to enjoy the ambiance of garden foliage all year round, you need to find the very best plants. If you want a low maintenance plant and /or plants, here are few great ideas:

### *The Bromeliad*

is a beautiful plant with a bright, fragrant bloom that lasts longer than the blossoming period of a typical plant, whether the plant is indoors or whether it Is outdoors. It will easily enhance the overall décor and ambiance of your home - you will immediately be able to see the difference an indoor garden can make.

### *The snake plant*

is another great example of easy maintenance indoor gardening plant. The only real problem that most people with snake plants face is death by over watering. That makes it nice. You don't have to remember to water as often as you would with other kinds of plants. The snake plant has very low light requirements than many other plants indoors and outdoors - therefore, it is suitable for virtually any indoor environment.

# *Indoor Gardening Supplies*

In order to thrive, we humans need air to breath, light to see by and food to nourish our bodies. Try thinking of the plant in the same way. It is a living, growing thing that needs those three elements in order to thrive.

Some different indoor garden supplies will be needed since you are in complete control of the growing process; ***the soil mixture, the water and nutrients and the placement of the indoor garden.*** Two different methods of indoor gardening can be employed. Plants in pots, in soil or a hydroponic garden on water and nutrients alone. Hydroponic gardening supplies are completely different than the conventional ones.

For a conventional indoor garden, the size of the pots, the soil you will be using are dictated by the plant. There are different kinds of potting soils on the market, some with fertilisers already in them, some heavier than the others with a different pH.

The next thing on the list of your indoor gardening supplies is a ***base for the pot*** to absorb the overflow. You should decide on the location before you fill the pot. It will be much heavier to move later.

### *The light the plant*

will get is another matter. Since there is not enough sun indoors, you will most probably need to buy special lights that duplicate the spectrum of the sun. Those can be found in indoor gardening supplies stores or on the Internet. When using lights, the matter of heat has to be taken into consideration. The light (unless it is an LED light) emits heat and if it is too close to the plants it might dry them up. In this case you need to use ventilation to cool down the light itself or use a reflector with a cooling system.

### *Watering Can*

Next on the list of indoor gardening supplies is a simple but good watering can, one that is not too heavy when empty and one that sprays the water evenly across a bigger area.

Since your plant doesn't have decomposing matter like a garden soil has, adding nutrients is very important. Those can be found in any nursery among the indoor gardening supplies.

These are all the indoor garden supplies you will need for healthy, thriving indoors plants and a green lush indoor garden.
