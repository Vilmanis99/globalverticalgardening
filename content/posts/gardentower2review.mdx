### How to Use Garden Tower 2

Its quite simple to use, just follow this simple step;

  Set up your Container

  Fill with soil and small amount of organic waste

Add red wiggler worms

  Add new kitchen waste to the bin each day

  Water your plants

  Excess fertilizer, especially in the form of worm juice, collects in a tray at the bottom of the tower. Remove this tray to spread the nutrient-dense juice on your plants.

  Recycle this excess water before pouring it on the soil at the top of the tower. The entire cycle starts over again.
