## Lettuce

![Hydroponic vegetables list](/images/hydroponic-vegetables-list/Lettuce.jpg)

Crunchy and crispy Lettuce is the best salad ingredient for your hydroponic gardening that requires less space than cauliflower and fewer nutrients. To grow lettuce, you don’t have to add many minerals or nutrients because this plant abstracts nutrients itself from the water. But if you want to boost the process of growing for whole year harvesting, then the addition of nutrientsis must.

### Nutritional Facts Of Lettuce

From the king salad family, this crispy vegetable is one of the best-known diet eatables. Growing lettuce with the hydroponic system could provide you multiple health benefits, such as it contains vitamins like A, C, and K and minerals rich in iron, calcium, and magnesium for the essential body metabolism.

You can eat twice a day for healthy body weight, either to reduce or gain mass.

## Spinach

![spinach](/images/hydroponic-vegetables-list/Spinach.jpg)
